Project Ummah Privacy Policy

At Project Ummah, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information through our website, located at projectummah.com.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other reasons. We will post the updated Privacy Policy on our website and will indicate the date of the last revision.
